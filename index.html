Who We Are?

We are engaged in manufacturing of Gold/Silver special electroplating chemicals. All chemicals are cyanide free and non-hazardous to skin.

SILV

For Silver Palting By Pen/Brush

CORONA

For Silver/Gold Cyanide Bath

AGO

For Gold Plating By Pen/Brush and Bath

SILV

  • It can be used for silver plating of huge items like Kalash, Mugut, etc. of any size.
  • It gives very good bonding to coating.
  • 100 gm. of SPC can be dissolved in 1 litre of SILV.
  • 1 gm. of SPC covers 1 sq. ft. area.
  • A rectifier of 3V/2A is required for plating.
  • Steel strip or rod can be used as a pen/brush.
  • Requires much less capital invest.

CORONA

  • An additive for cyanide bath
  • It gives very good bonding to coating.
  • Only 30-60 ml of CORONA needs to be added per litre
  • Low current density and low voltage is required (2-3 Volts)
  • It also adds heat resistance to coating
  • It may increase the rate of deposition
  • Plating can be done with the help of only Steel anode, instead of silver anode in conventional plating techniques
  • Requires much less capital investment
  • Plating up to 15-20 microns or even more can be done, as and when required
  • Only 30 gm. of cyanide/ litre is required
  • Only 2-3 gm. of SPC per litre needs to be added
  • Operating cost of this technique is much less as compared to conventional plating techniques
  • Iron can be directly silver plated without any undercoat
  • Only one time addition

AGO - By Pen/Brush

  • It can be used for gold plating of huge items like Kalash, Mugut, etc. of any size.
  • It gives very good bonding to coating
  • 100 gm. of GPC can be dissolved in 1 litre of AGO
  • 1 gm. of GPC covers 1 sq. ft. area
  • A rectifier of 3V/2A is required for plating
  • Steel strip or rod can be used as a pen/brush
  • Requires much less capital investment

AGO - By Bath

  • Gold platting on copper/silver/gold jewellery
  • It gives very good bonding to coating
  • Add 2-3 gms GPC to 1 litter AGO
  • It is a completely Cyanide free chemical
  • Steel Anode to be used
  • 3 volt and 0.01 amp/sq.cm to be applied
  • Requires much less capital investment
